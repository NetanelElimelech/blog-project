John Welsh

Professor at MIT

Born: 11.11.1964

Nationality: USA

In Brief:

Some people can find it difficult to brag about themselves, but it is an important professional skill. You don’t have to come across as being big-headed but you do need to be able to highlight your accomplish­ments and achieve­ments because it is these that will attract people to use your services, hire you for a position, or otherwise engage with you.
